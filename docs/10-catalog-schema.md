# Catalog Database Schema

Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

---

# Purpose

The Catalog domain manages all commerce-related entities.

This includes:

- Products
- Categories
- Brands
- Product Images
- Product Attributes
- Product Variants (future-ready)
- Product Relationships
- Product Translations

The Catalog domain intentionally excludes SEO-specific data.

SEO is handled by the dedicated SEO domain.

---
